Ship Ahoy! 

Navigating away from limited visibility, to unveil the environmental pressures and impacts from maritime transport
 

 




This application was developed as part of an InfraVis visualisation support project. 

The visualisations were performed on resources provided by InfraVis under Swedish Research Council grant 2021-00181. 

 

PI: Ida-Maja Hassellöv  
Full Professor, Head of Division, Maritime Environmental Science, Environmental and Energy Sciences
