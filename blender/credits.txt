Ship Ahoy!

Navigating away from limited visibility, to unveil the environmental pressures and impacts from maritime transport.

This application was developed as part of an InfraVis visualisation support project. PI: Ida-Maja Hassellöv

Explore more at infravis.github.io/shipAhoy
